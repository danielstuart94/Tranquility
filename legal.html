Privacy & Terms

Plain‑English summary. Prototype only. Obtain legal review before production.

What we collect

  • Anonymous slider settings and weights on the demo.
  • One‑tap agreement (“Yes/No”) and optional comment.
  • Timestamp and site identifier (e.g., Lyric Square).

No names, emails, precise location, or third‑party cookies. A short anonymous session hash may be stored.

Consent

Telemetry sends only if the “allow anonymous feedback” box is ticked. You can untick it at any time.

Use of data

  • Improve the Tranquility Index model.
  • Aggregate insights on seasons, comfort, and events.
  • Evaluate pilots with public‑realm partners.

Retention

Raw events up to 12 months, aggregates longer. You can request deletion referencing your session hash (if available).

Cookies & storage

No third‑party cookies. LocalStorage is used for your slider settings.

Third‑party services

The demo calls Open‑Meteo for weather and air quality. Their terms apply.
