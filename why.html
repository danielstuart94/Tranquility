Why Tqind Matters

Tqind started with acousticians in Hammersmith who wanted better lunch-break spots and better WFH walks. We built a score that tells you, simply, where to go and when.

The problem we solve

  • People need clear signals: quiet enough to call, enough shade, cleaner air, a place to sit.
  • Councils and developers need proof their spending improves real experience.
  • Businesses need outdoor areas that make people stay longer.

What improves a score

  • Reduce traffic noise. Add planting and shade. Provide seating and cover.
  • Manage peak footfall. Activate space without overwhelming it.
  • Maintain clean air and thermal comfort where possible.

Commercial value

  • Councils: evidence for funding and planning. Targeted upgrades.
  • Developers: marketing edge; potential value premium near high-score spaces.
  • Employers: healthier staff, lower absence, higher productivity.
  • Operators/brands: outdoor areas that attract and retain customers.
