Why Tqind

Started by acousticians in Hammersmith to find better lunch‑break spots and better WFH walks. Now a city‑scale signal for outdoor quality.

Problem

  • People need clear signals: quiet enough to call, shade, cleaner air, a place to sit.
  • Cities need proof spend improves real experience.
  • Businesses need outdoor areas that make people stay.

How we score

Seven factors with transparent weights. Hourly profiles plus on‑site validation during beta.

Value

  • Councils: evidence for bids and planning.
  • Developers: marketing edge near high‑score spaces.
  • Employers: healthier staff use nearby outdoor spaces.
